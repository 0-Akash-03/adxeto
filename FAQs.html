FAQs

    • Do you provide only Retargeting services or cold ads are also included?

    • We only provide retargeting services, it does not include cold ads/campaigns. but you can request for cold ads differently. and then that will include both cold & retargeting ad campaigns.

    • What's the difference b/w cold campaigns & retargeting campaigns?

    • When we run cold campaigns, we reach to a new audience, that might not be yet aware of your product. But with Retargeting campaigns, we target your existing website audience (for example, one might visit your website's pricing page but did not come back, then we could retarget them and explain why they should work with you or purchase your subscription.)

    • Is this service only for SaaS or similar companies also could work?

    • This retargeting service is mainly crafted for SaaS companies. But if you operate same as any other SaaS company, you can surely use our service, the process would be similar.

    • Can I leverage your service as a new startup with very less website visits?

    • No. If you do not have a good traffic, it won't be wise to work with us at this stage. Though, In case you want to increase traffic with cold ads, you can request for that differently.

    • What's the minimum budget required for this work?

    • The minimum budget will always depend on you. Though, we suggest to have atleast $500 or $1000/mo campaign budget for a decent result. Ofcourse, You can go quite higher than this.

    • Do you have a free or paid trial?

    • No, we do not have any free or paid trial. In extreme cases, If you wanted to try us before working on full month, you can have paid trial differently. ( but it's only for limited companies, not for all )
